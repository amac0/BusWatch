BusWatch Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

BusWatch is a Wear OS application that shows real-time London bus arrivals. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we access and how we use it.

Data We Access

Location Data

BusWatch requests access to your device's location to find bus stops near you. This location data is:

  • Used locally only - Your location is sent directly to the Transport for London (TfL) API to find nearby stops
  • Not stored - We do not store your location history
  • Not shared - Your location is not shared with any third parties other than TfL's public API
  • Not transmitted to our servers - BusWatch has no backend servers; all data flows directly between your device and TfL

Network Data

The app connects to the TfL Unified API to retrieve:

  • Bus stop information (names, locations, routes)
  • Real-time arrival predictions

Data We Store

BusWatch stores minimal data on your device only:

  • Last selected stop - To quickly show your preferred stop when you open the app
  • Preferences - Any app settings you configure

This data is stored locally on your watch and is not transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

Transport for London (TfL) API

BusWatch uses the TfL Unified API to retrieve bus information. When you use BusWatch, your approximate location is sent to TfL to find nearby stops. TfL's privacy policy applies to their processing of this data: TfL Privacy Policy

Google Play Services

BusWatch uses Google Play Services for location functionality. Google's privacy policy applies: Google Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

BusWatch does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app simply displays public transport information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
